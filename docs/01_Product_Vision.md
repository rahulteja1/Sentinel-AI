# 1. Product Vision

Sentinel AI Platform is an enterprise-grade, multi-agent artificial intelligence platform designed to unify information retrieval, workflow automation, knowledge management, and operational support into a single intelligent workspace for law enforcement agencies.

Instead of requiring personnel to navigate multiple disconnected systems, Sentinel AI provides a centralized platform where specialized AI agents collaborate to retrieve information, organize knowledge, assist with documentation, and support investigative workflows.

The platform is designed to augment human capabilities while ensuring that all operational decisions remain under human control.

---

# 2. Product Goals

The primary goals of Sentinel AI are:

- Reduce administrative workload
- Improve information accessibility
- Accelerate report preparation
- Support investigative workflows
- Improve knowledge discovery
- Standardize access to departmental policies
- Demonstrate enterprise AI architecture
- Showcase modern software engineering practices

---

# 3. Product Philosophy

Sentinel AI is built on the following principles.

## AI Assists People

AI should support officers and analysts—not replace professional judgment.

---

## Enterprise First

Every component should be modular, scalable, secure, and maintainable.

---

## Human Oversight

Critical actions always require human review and approval.

---

## Explainability

Every AI-generated response should be traceable to supporting information whenever possible.

---

## Open Architecture

The platform should allow new agents and services to be added without redesigning the entire system.

# 9. Non-Functional Requirements

Performance

Average response time under 5 seconds for standard queries.

Scalability

Support additional AI agents without architectural redesign.

Availability

Local deployment with optional future cloud deployment.

Maintainability

Modular services with clearly defined interfaces.

Security

Authentication, authorization, and audit logging built into the platform.

# 12. Long-Term Vision

Sentinel AI aims to demonstrate how enterprise multi-agent systems can improve information access, workflow efficiency, and knowledge management for public safety organizations through open-source technologies and modern software architecture.

The project serves as a reference implementation for enterprise AI engineering rather than a production operational system.
